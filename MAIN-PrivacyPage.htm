Privacy

The privacy policy is to provide information to you on how personal information is collected and used within the Online Medical Centre, and the scenarios in which we may share it with third parties.

Why do we collect, use, store and share your personal information?

The Online Medical Centre will need to collect your personal information in order to provide healthcare services to you. Our primary intention for gathering, using, storing and sharing your personal details is to manage your health. We also must use it for business activities, such as financial payments and transactions or practice accreditation and audits.

What kinds of personal information are required for collection?

The information we will collect from you includes:

  • Contact details, names, date of birth & home addresses
  • Medical information, such as:
    1. Medical history
    2. Family history
    3. Risk factors
    4. Medication
    5. Allergies
    6. Immunisations
    7. Adverse events (fainting, broken bones, etc.)
  • Healthcare identifiers
  • Medicare numbers - for claiming and identification purposes
