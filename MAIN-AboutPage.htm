About

The Online Medical Centre is a completely free website providing remote consultations with top-notch doctors.

We understand that safety is the number one priority for all patients, that’s why we felt there needed to be an application to facilitate remote doctor’s appointments. Whether elderly, disabled or incapacitated, this service provides remote consulations with qualified doctors and specialists. Through these private consultations, you are able to be treated, receive diagnosis and collect prescriptions, all within the comfort of your own home.

The Online Medical Centre is completely free for users. Howver, donations and crowd-fund help maintain the website.
