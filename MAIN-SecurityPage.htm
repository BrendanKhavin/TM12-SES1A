Security

The Online Medical Centre encrypts your personal details and uses secure servers for your payment. This means that any information provided to us will be in safe hands and remain private, including your debit and credit card details. We use Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology with 128-bit encryption. This ensures that you are getting the highest possible protection when you make transactions over the Internet. Your debit/credit card payment is processed by CommBank Transact API, which has its own 128-bit SSL security certificate. Also, whenever you make a financial transaction online, you should always keep a look out for a padlock symbol on your browser. This demonstrates that the page you are on is stable and secure, and you can complete your transaction with absolute confidence.
